Karin Hoelzl is an actor, writer, and producer originally from Virginia. Her theater journey started in her grandparents’ barn, performing musicals with handmade costumes—the rest has been icing. Karin is especially passionate about new work development and physical theater, with a focus on making art more accessible to contemporary audiences.
